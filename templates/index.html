This is the Leo-o-matic quiz generator. To get a quiz, send a GET request to /make_quiz with these arguments:
  • q: the question
  • o: a semicolon separated list of options
  • c: the index of the correct option (from 0)
Alternatively, you can use this form to generate the request. Upon submission, you will be automatically redirected to the quiz itself, and you can copy and paste the url to embed it in Notion.
